Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026  |  Last updated: July 8, 2026

Morton Roofing ("Morton Roofing," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website at www.mortonroofing.best and mortonroofing.best (collectively, the "Site"), contact us by phone or email, or submit a request through our online forms.

By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

1. Who We Are

Morton Roofing is a roofing contractor and public adjusting company based in Illinois. We provide residential and commercial roofing, gutters, siding, storm damage assessment, and insurance claim assistance throughout Chicagoland and surrounding communities.

  • Business name: Morton Roofing
  • Phone: 331-481-3708
  • Email: public.adjustingoffice@gmail.com
  • Service area: Illinois, including Woodridge, Naperville, Bolingbrook, Joliet, Calumet City, and surrounding areas

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out our free inspection form, contact us by email, or communicate with us by phone, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property address
  • Messages or details you choose to share about storm damage, roofing needs, or insurance claims
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, certain technical information may be collected automatically by our hosting and security providers, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Site
  • Referring website or search terms
  • Date and time of access

We do not currently use advertising cookies or third-party marketing trackers on the Site. Our hosting provider, Cloudflare, may process limited technical data to deliver the Site securely and protect against abuse.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and inspection requests
  • Schedule property evaluations and provide roofing or public adjusting services
  • Communicate with you about your project, claim, or appointment
  • Coordinate with your insurance company when you have engaged us for claim-related services
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and unauthorized access

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party services to operate the Site and deliver communications. This includes Cloudflare (website hosting and security) and FormSubmit (form delivery to our email inbox). These providers process data on our behalf and only as needed to perform their services.
  • Insurance and project partners: If you hire us for insurance claim or restoration work, we may share relevant property and claim information with your insurance carrier, adjusters, suppliers, or subcontractors as necessary to complete your project — and only with your knowledge or as authorized by you.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, subpoena, or government request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business transfers: If Morton Roofing is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

5. Form Submissions & Email Delivery

When you submit our online inspection request form, your information is transmitted through FormSubmit and delivered to our business email at public.adjustingoffice@gmail.com. FormSubmit acts as a data processor that helps route form submissions to us. We encourage you not to submit sensitive information (such as Social Security numbers, full insurance policy numbers, or financial account details) through the online form. If sensitive documents are needed, we will collect them through secure channels during our direct engagement with you.

6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

The Site does not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking at this time. Cloudflare and your browser may use strictly necessary technical mechanisms to deliver pages, maintain security, and ensure the Site functions properly. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may affect Site functionality.

7. Third-Party Links & Services

The Site may contain links to third-party websites or services (for example, Google Fonts for typography loading). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

Third-party services used by the Site may include:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, CDN, and security (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)
  • FormSubmit — form processing and email delivery (FormSubmit Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — web font delivery (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Trustindex — Google reviews widget display

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to respond to inquiries, provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. Inquiry and project-related records may be kept for several years where required for insurance, contracting, or accounting purposes.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, which may include the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal exceptions
  • Opt out of certain processing where applicable

Illinois residents and residents of other states with privacy laws may have additional rights under applicable state law. To exercise any privacy right, contact us using the information in Section 14 below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and as required by applicable law.

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

11. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

12. Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. Because there is no uniform industry standard for responding to such signals, the Site does not currently respond to Do Not Track browser settings.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be noted on the Site. Your continued use of the Site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
